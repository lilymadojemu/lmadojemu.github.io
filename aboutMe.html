About Me

Hi, I’m Grace Garmo! I’m a Senior at the University of Michigan School of Information studying UX Design and Research. My love for UX + product design began far before I ever heard the term “User Experience”. For as long as I can remember, I’ve been fascinated by human behavior from the lens of technology. This fostered my passion for human-computer interaction. I spent my free time doodling redesigns of my favorite apps on napkins. I kept a piece of scratch paper with a list of websites my friends enjoyed so I could check them out and figure out what made them good. I’ve been a UX designer my entire life — I just didn’t know it.
